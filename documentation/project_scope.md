# Project Scope

## Project Title
USA Wildfire Analytics

## Purpose
Analyze historical wildfire activity across the United States to identify trends in wildfire frequency, severity, geographic distribution, causes, and land ownership patterns.


## Stakeholder Perspective

This analysis could support:
- Environmental agencies and non-profits
- Emergency management organizations
- Policy makers
- Resource planning teams

## Dataset 

## Dataset

Source:
Kaggle - 1.88 Million US Wildfires

Short, Karen C. 2017. Spatial wildfire occurrence data for the United States, 1992-2015 [FPA_FOD_20170508]. 4th Edition. Fort Collins, CO: Forest Service Research Data Archive. https://doi.org/10.2737/RDS-2013-0009.4


Time Period:
1992-2015

Observations:
Approximately 1.88 million wildfire records

Primary Analysis Areas:
- Wildfire frequency over time
- Geographic patterns
- Fire severity
- Causes of wildfires
- Land ownership impacts
